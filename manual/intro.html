Train Celes from a rank novice into an expert
wizard. However, training is not cheap, and without
being able to produce mana on her own, you will need
to make time in your schedule to ensure that she has
enough mana to even be able to practice magic at all,
let alone use it in battle.

You have just three years to prove her worth!

Training
Train Celes how to defend herself with a variety of weapons and combat styles.
Study Magic
Teach Celes spells from seven different schools of magic.
Adventure
Take part in local tournaments and climb to the top of monster filled towers.
Part Time Job
Earn money to pay for your training and to buy new equipment while honing your skills
Rest
Recover your stamina, health, mana, and refresh the spirit.
