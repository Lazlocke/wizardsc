Weekly Activities
Set Celes's schedule for the week to increase her 15 attributes.
Additional activites are unlocked as you progress through the game.

ÅüTraining
Activity Description
Physical Training Exercise to increase Celes's Physical Strength.
Martial Arts Practice martial arts to increase Celes's Unarmed capabilities.
Weapons Training Learn how to fight at short range and train Celes's Melee Weapons capabilities.
Distance Training Learn how to fight at range and train Celes's Ranged Weapons capabilities.
Unconventional Learn how to fight with unusual weapons like whips and barrels, increasing Celes's Unconventional capability.

ÅüStudy Magic
Activity Description
Magical Study Study the fundamentals of magic and increase Celes's Wizardry capability.
Pyromancy Study Study the art of the flame and raise Celes's Pyromancy capability.
Hydromancy Study Study water magic and increase Celes's Hydromancy capability.
Aeromancy Study Study wind magic and increase Celes's Aeromancy capability.
Geomancy Study Study earth magic and increase Celes's Geomancy capability.
Electromancy Study Study lightning magic and increase Celes's Electromancy capability.
Umbramancy Study Study dark magic and increase Celes's Umbramancy capability.
Photomancy Study Study light magic and increase Celes's Photomancy capability.

ÅüPart Time Job
Activity Description
Waitress Work as a waitress for a small amount of money.
Adventurer Join an adventurer party for a share of the rewards, but combat may occur, and the cost of provisions may outweigh the pay.
Line Cook Work as a chef at a local restaurant for a moderate amount of pay, if you're a good enough cook, that is.
Watchman Work as a night watchman, but you may have to fight troublemakers, and will be responsible for the damages if they get away.
Tournament Staff Only available the last week of each month, but pays decently and gives Celes combat tips.
Research Assistant A low paying job for a novice mage that raises a variety of magical capabilities.
Housework Won't earn much money, but does increase Celes's General Studies capability.
Personal Assistant Assist Lazlocke in his work. Pays almost nothing, but hard to fail at.
Seasonal Work In the spring and fall, fields need to be worked, and in the summer, beaches need lifeguarding.

ÅüAdventure
Activity Description
Wizards Tournament Make a name for yourself and compete in the local tournament at the end of every month.
Coudures Qualifiers Held twice a year at the end at the end of June and end of December. You need to win this tournament to make the finals.
Climb a Tower There are numerous towers filled with monsters, traps, and treasures for Celes to explore and conquer.

ÅüRest
Activity Description
Shopping Recover a small amount of HP and visit the local stores. Some weeks, there are special bargains.
Sleeping Just take it easy for a week, sleep in, and recover a large amount of HP.
Reading Leisurely read, recovering a moderate amount of HP and slightly increasing a few capabilities.
Fun with Master Enjoy a carefree week, playing around with Lazlocke.
Recover Mana Spend a week concentrating on transfering mana from Lazlocke to Celes.
