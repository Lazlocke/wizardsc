Tournaments
There are a number of tournaments Celes can compete in.
Some are secret unless you're an expert in the field.
Victory in tournaments will unlock new events and new challenges.
Face off against your rivals, and even some of the greatest mages in the world!

Tournament Description
Queensbell Magic Tournament A tournament held at the end of each month in Loria for up and coming wizards to make a name for themselves.
Coudures Qualifiers Held in December every three years. Winning here is a must to reach the finals.
Coudures Tournament of Wizards Held in January once every three years. Weeds out the best from the best, but you need to win in the qualifiers to be invited.
Bancaster Martial Arts Tournament Held in February once every four years. Only experienced wizards who have won another tournament are invited.
Tournament of True Heroes A very unorthodox competition held in Septemer every other year, but only tournament winners are invited.
Loria Cookoff A competition for chefs, not combatants. Held in June, every other year, but only talented chefs and those dedicated to working in culinary fields are invited.

Towers
Climb to the top of various towers and achieve greatness.
Treasures, traps, monsters, and adventure all await Celes.

Tower Description
Tower of Certification A very tall tower that starts easy, but eventually becomes nearly impossible. Used for assigning official ranks to wizards.
Tower of Trials A tower for novice wizards to test their strength, but don't underestimate it.
Tower of Magic A tower for more advanced wizards after they've conquered the Tower of Trials.
Tower of Blessings A very tower for experienced wizards, but is full of blessings to assist in the climb.
Tower of Despair A far away tower that needs flight magic to reach. Full of traps, especially those that sap mana.
Tower of the Underlord A tower for experienced wizrads, full of traps and monsters. Only those who have conquered the Tower of Magic will hear of it.
Tower of Towers A tower for experienced adventurers and tournament winners. Only rich wizards are invited. Full of hordes of enemies, not to mention ancient dragons.
Tower of Twilight A tower full of angels and demons. You'll have to be a very experienced adventurer who has conquered a true dungeon to find this.
Tower of Arias The official Wizard's Guild tower, full of the strongest wizards in the city. You need a personal invitation from the leader of the Guild to challenge it.
Labyrinth A very distant underground tower. Celes will need considerable flight magic to reach this monster-filled dungeon.
The Great Labyrinth Celes will need to be a master of flight magic to reach this trap and monster filled dungeon, but it's full of treasure too.
Overlord's Castle This castle is located on the opposite side of the world, so only a master of flying magic can reach it. Full of rare and powerful demons, and treasures.
